To start using EagleEYE you will first need to make sure you have a personal EagleEYE account.
To do this you will need to Sign Up.

There are 2 options when Signing Up:

1. If Your business is new to EagleEYE

If your business is new to EagleEYE you will first need to setup the new EagleEYE Business Account.
This will create a new database to store all the information your business needs.
Along with the database, the Business Account will require you to create a Business Manager Account. This account will have full administrative capabilites of your Business Account.

Step 1: Click "Sign Up"


Step 2: Click "Create New Database and Administrator"


Step 3: Complete all fields and then Click "Create New Business DB and Admin Account".

You are now ready to Login !



2. If You are a new Employee of an existing EagleEYE Business Account

If you are a new Employee that needs to be added to an existing EagleEYE Business Account, then follow these steps:


Step 1: Click "Sign Up"


Step 2: Click "Create New Employee"


Step 3: Complete all fields and choose your Position.
*Make sure the Business Database you entered is the same as the EagleEYE Business Account name.

You are now ready to Login !
